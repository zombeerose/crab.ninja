--How do you know what you should be when you grow up?--

How does anybody truly decide what they should be when they grow up?  For starters, don't confuse this question with what do they *want* to be?

Everybody is great at something given enough practice, dedication, and effort.  But if you aren't pointed in the right direction and the Irish luck doesn't favor you, then most people flounder around their whole life without knowing greatness.
